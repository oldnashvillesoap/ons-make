Application Documentation

Overview

ArtisanOS is designed to help small-batch producers manage recipes, track inventory with FIFO costing, and plan production efficiently.

Recipes Tab

Create recipes with ingredients and yields. Costs are calculated automatically based on current inventory prices and update as material prices change.

Inventory & FIFO Costing

Log materials with costs. The system uses FIFO (First-In, First-Out) to consume oldest inventory first, ensuring realistic cost tracking and material flow.

Production Batches

Plan batches for future production, then execute them when ready. Executing locks in costs and deducts materials from inventory using FIFO.

Forecasting & Alerts

The system projects material needs from planned batches and alerts you if inventory is insufficient to meet your production goals.
